City Cataclysm

A Team Deathmatch Map
made with Unreal Engine 4

  • Watch Trailer
Learn More

Level Abstract

Set in a near future urban setting, utilizing
verticality to create an interestng Team Deathmatch (TDM)
experience similar to more abstract level designs.

  • 16 Players

  • Classic TDM

  • Futuristic Urban

Map Trailer

Walkthrough with Commentary

Mid Section

Major choke- and focal point of the map.

Paths

Simple map design with three major choke points.
Connector pahts between the choke points and
multiple levels of verticality adds flow and
complexity to the gameplay.

Map Cutoffs

Cutoffs in the map have been designed in such a way as to not
give the player the feel of arbitrarily being locked out
and hence break immersion.

Corridors and Connectors

Attention has been spent on making corridors not be tedious
and bland and always be open.
